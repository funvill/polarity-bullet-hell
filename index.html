POLARITY

It's all about your polarity

  • Right Mouse Button to change polarity (Black/White)
  • Absorb bullets of the same polarity to increase energy
  • Your bullets only harm ships of different polarity (black bullets hurt white ships)
  • When your energy is full, use the Space Bar to trigger bomb
  • Left Mouse Button to fire main weapon
  • High score wins
